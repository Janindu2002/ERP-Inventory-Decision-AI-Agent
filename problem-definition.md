# Problem Definition

## Background

In many organizations, inventory management decisions are still made manually by reviewing ERP reports.

Managers often need to analyze large datasets to determine:

- Which products are running low
- When to reorder inventory
- Which suppliers offer the best cost or delivery time
- Which products have increasing demand

This process is time-consuming and prone to human error.

---

## Problem Statement

Manual inventory analysis creates several challenges:

- Delayed reorder decisions
- Risk of stock shortages
- Inefficient supplier selection
- Lack of demand insights
- Increased operational workload

Businesses need an automated way to analyze inventory data and provide decision support.

---

## Proposed Solution

Develop an **AI-powered Inventory Decision Assistant** that:

1. Reads ERP inventory reports (CSV or PDF)
2. Analyzes stock levels and reorder thresholds
3. Evaluates sales rates and demand
4. Identifies products requiring reorder
5. Recommends optimal suppliers
6. Automatically generates reorder invoices

---

## Expected Benefits

- Faster decision making
- Reduced stock shortages
- Improved supplier selection
- Data-driven inventory planning
- Reduced manual workload
